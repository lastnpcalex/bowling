Bowling Analysis

Welcome to the Bowling Game Analysis tool. Follow these steps to analyze your game data:

  1. Click the Choose Files button below to select one or more JSON files exported from your bowling simulator.
  2. Once your files are selected, click Analyze to start the analysis process.
  3. View the results, including your average score, strike percentage, and spare percentage, after the analysis completes.
  4. Click Generate Representative Game to see a scorecard based on your statistics.
